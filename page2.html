Reader Survey

One of my favorite activities to do when I'm not working on my class website is to read. In order to keep track of the books that I've read, I keep track of them with Goodreads. With Goodreads, I can keep track of the titles of the books I've read, what I thought of it, and how many I've read.

Now that I go to school and work full-time, I don't have as much time as I would like to read books. That is, unless you count non-fiction books, such as my textbooks.

My favorite genres are: literary fiction, dystopia, science fiction, and thrillers. My least favorites are romance and political books.

I thought it would be interesting to take a survey of readers to find out more about their reading preferences. By responding, you will be entered into a contest to receive a free library card. Ha ha! What did you expect? I'm a college student. I don't have any money to spend on incentives.
